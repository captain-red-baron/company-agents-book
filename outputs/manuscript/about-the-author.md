# About the Author

Dr. Marcel Müller holds a PhD in business process management and has spent years building at the intersection of systems, software, and entrepreneurship. He has led teams inside large companies and founded and scaled his own software businesses.

That combination of academic rigor and operating experience shapes his view of what comes next. He believes the company of the future will be built around one human coordinating dozens of agents, using a delegation model that is surprisingly close to how companies have always worked: a human sets direction, assigns work, and manages execution across specialized contributors.

This book is an exploration of that shift and of what becomes possible when agent management becomes a core business capability.

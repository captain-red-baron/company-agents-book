# What a Company Does

A company does more than sell products. At a deeper level, it coordinates specialized work, turns assets and knowledge into output, channels decision-making, and creates an accountability system that makes scale possible.

### Working Synthesis

If you ask what a company really does beneath the surface, the answer is that it reduces chaos. It bundles people, capital, knowledge, and authority into a structure that can repeatedly decide, execute, learn, and remain accountable over time.

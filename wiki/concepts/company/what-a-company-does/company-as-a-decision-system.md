# Company as a Decision System

Companies do not only coordinate work. They also decide under uncertainty. A company determines where attention goes, who has authority, what tradeoffs count, and how choices are made when information is incomplete.

## Sections

### Core Idea

Herbert Simon's [Administrative Behavior](https://mitpressbookstore.mit.edu/book/9780684835822) places decision-making at the heart of administration. James March and Herbert Simon's [Organizations](https://www.wiley-vch.de/en/areas-interest/finance-economics-law/business-management-13ba/organization-management-theory-13ba2/organizational-behavior-13ba21/organizations-978-0-631-18631-1) expands this by showing how organizations deal with bounded rationality, conflict, participation, communication, and planning. In this view, a company is partly a machine for structuring attention and choice.

### Details

This lens is helpful because it explains why companies exist even when everybody involved is intelligent and motivated. The problem is not only effort. It is limited attention, imperfect information, conflicting goals, and the need to make decisions before certainty arrives.

Simon and March help show that organizations are devices for handling bounded rationality. They simplify reality, set roles, define who decides what, determine which decisions are routine and which require escalation, and create channels through which information becomes action.

For entrepreneurs, this matters a lot. A growing company is partly a scaling decision system. If the founder remains the bottleneck for every important call, the company eventually stalls. The AI-agent version of this question is whether agents can expand the effective decision bandwidth of one human without destroying judgment quality.

### Why It Matters

This matters for the agent future because agents can help execute decisions, but they do not remove the need for objective-setting, escalation paths, and judgment. The question shifts from whether decisions exist to how the decision system is redesigned.

# How a Company Is Built

A company is not built from headcount alone. It is built from layers: a legal shell, governance rules, organizational structure, repeatable processes, and capabilities that improve through use.

### Working Synthesis

The practical architecture of a company starts with who the company is in law and ends with what the company can do repeatedly in practice. That gap between formal structure and operating capability is where most companies either become real or remain fragile.

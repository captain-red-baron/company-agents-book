# About the Author

Dr. Marcel Müller brings together academic depth in business process management with long-term operating experience as an entrepreneur, software founder, and team leader. His perspective on AI-native companies is grounded in both theory and execution: he believes the next generation of companies will be built around one human coordinating dozens of agents.

## Sections

### Background

Dr. Marcel Müller holds a PhD in business process management and has spent years working at the intersection of systems, software, and company building. He has led teams inside large organizations and has also built and scaled his own software businesses.

### Point of View

His core argument is that the company of the future will not look fundamentally alien. Instead, it will follow a familiar organizational logic: one human leader delegating work across many specialized agents, much like managers have traditionally coordinated teams inside companies.

### Relevance to This Book

This combination of academic training and entrepreneurial practice shapes the central thesis of the book. The book explores how humans can use agent systems not as novelties, but as a new operating layer for execution, scale, and leverage.
